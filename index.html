The Weekend at a Glance
Thursday April 30
Everyone arrives and settles into the Airbnb. Afternoon shopping in the Uptown Design District with Lorenn, then VillageFest street fair on Palm Canyon Drive — food, vendors, and live performers. Dinner at Tac/Quila on Palm Canyon, then games night back at the house.
Friday May 1
Lazy morning, pool, games, and pre-game drinks at the Airbnb. Into your Desert Disco outfits and out the door by 5:30. Five-stop pub crawl: dinner at Birba, rooftop drinks at High Bar, tropical cocktails at The Tropicale, drinks at GiGi's firepit, and dancing at Village Pub until 2am.
Saturday May 2
Slow recovery morning at the Airbnb — no rush, no alarm. French garden brunch at Farm in La Plaza courtyard. Ace Hotel pool party through the afternoon. Back to get changed, then dinner at Clandestino on the patio under the Marilyn Monroe statue.
Sunday May 3
Sleep in, coffee on the patio, no agenda. Breakfast cooked together with the last of the groceries. Pack up, hugs at the door, and safe travels home. The desert misses you already.

Stop One  ·  Dinner
Birba  —  The Bride's Choice
Farm-to-table Italian on a beautiful heat-lamp patio. Wood-fired pizza, black pepper tagliatelle, fried squash blossom. Start the night well fed. Book ahead — this one fills up fast. birbaf10.com  ·  (760) 327-5678
7:30 pm
Stop Two  ·  Drinks
Azucar at La Serena Villas
Rooftop bar inside a historic 1933 boutique hotel. Boho vibes, mountain views, and some of the best cocktails in Palm Springs — order the blood orange margarita. 21+ only. azucarpalmsprings.com  ·  (844) 932-8044
9:00 pm
Stop Three  ·  Drinks
High Bar at the Kimpton Rowan
The highest rooftop bar in downtown Palm Springs. City lights on below you, mountains glowing — the best group photo moment of the night. rowanpalmsprings.com
10:30 pm
Stop Four  ·  Drinks
GiGi's
Tulum-meets-Palm Springs vibes at V Palm Springs — indoor/outdoor bar, firepit cocktail area, fun drinks and great energy. The Flamingo Spritz is worth ordering. gigispalmsprings.com  ·  (760) 980-7000
11:45 pm
Stop Five  ·  Dancing
Village Pub
Multiple dance floors, DJ until 2am. A Palm Springs nightlife institution. Small cover at the door. villagepubps.com

Farm — French Garden Brunch
The most beautiful brunch in Palm Springs. A French Provencal garden patio in La Plaza courtyard — morning glory flowers, string lights, French press coffee, proper crepes. Walk-ins only. Arrive by 10:30am and put your name on the list. farmpalmsprings.com  ·  (760) 322-2724
12:30 pm
Pool Party
Ace Hotel Swim Club
DJ, desert sun, great pool energy. Buy day passes online in advance, they sell out on weekends. Drinks run $23 and up. acehotelpalmsprings.com

7:30 pm
Dinner
Clandestino
The stylish, moody sister of Tac/Quila — even better as a proper sit-down dinner. Patio with views of the Marilyn Monroe statue. Beer tempura avocado bites, cochinita pibil, mezcal cocktails. Book ahead. clandestinopalmsprings.com  ·  (760) 699-6222
